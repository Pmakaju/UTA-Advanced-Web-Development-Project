Our exotic coffee from UTA Bakery and Coffee is imported directly from farmers then roasted locally at our home base in Montreal, Canada. The result is fresh and authentic flavours that you can taste in every sip. We offer Haitian coffee, Santo Domingo coffee, Jamaican Blue Mountain coffee, and our own special Trip to the Islands coffee blend—each boasting their own unique and bold flavours.

Browse our collection of fine, exotic coffees from the Caribbean and reach out to us with any questions by calling 1 (450) 812-2328 during our business hours. Additionally, feel free to fill out a form and we’ll get back to you shortly.
